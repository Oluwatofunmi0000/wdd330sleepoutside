## How to Use This Board

1. **Daily Standup:** Review cards in progress, move completed cards
2. **Weekly Review:** Assess milestone progress, adjust priorities
3. **Blockers:** Add comments immediately when stuck
4. **Collaboration:** @mention teammates or instructor for questions
5. **Archive:** Move completed cards to "Completed" list regularly
